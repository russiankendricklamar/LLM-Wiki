# Neural Tangent Kernel

The [[Neural Tangent Kernel]] (NTK) describes the evolution of wide neural networks during gradient descent. The kernel $\Theta(x, x') = \langle \nabla_\theta f(x;\theta), \nabla_\theta f(x';\theta) \rangle$ remains constant in the infinite-width limit, linearizing the dynamics.
