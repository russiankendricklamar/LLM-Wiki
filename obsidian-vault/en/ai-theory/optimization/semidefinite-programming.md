# Semidefinite Programming

[[Semidefinite Programming]] (SDP) optimizes a linear objective over the intersection of the cone of positive semidefinite matrices and an affine space: $\min \text{Tr}(CX)$ s.t. $\text{Tr}(A_i X) = b_i$ and $X \succeq 0$. Used in control and MAX-CUT relaxations.
