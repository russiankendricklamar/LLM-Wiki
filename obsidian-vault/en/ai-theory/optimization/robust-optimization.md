# Robust Optimization

[[Robust Optimization]] tackles parameter uncertainty by optimizing for the worst-case scenario within an uncertainty set $\mathcal{U}$. $\min_{x} \max_{u \in \mathcal{U}} f(x, u)$. It avoids overfitting in stochastic environments.
