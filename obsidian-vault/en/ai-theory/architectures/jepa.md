# JEPA

[[JEPA]] (Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture) proposed by Yann LeCun learns abstract representations by predicting the latent representation of missing parts of an input (e.g., in I-JEPA or V-JEPA) rather than reconstructing pixels.
