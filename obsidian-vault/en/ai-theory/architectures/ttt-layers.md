# TTT-Layers

[[TTT-Layers]] (Test-Time Training) for sequential modeling adapt the hidden state by treating it as a machine learning model that is updated via [[convex-optimization|gradient descent]] on the fly during inference, offering an alternative to RNNs and Transformers.
