## Overview
LSTMs and GRUs are recurrent neural network (RNN) variants designed to solve the vanishing gradient problem in standard RNNs by using gating mechanisms.

## Gating Mechanisms
- **LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory):** Uses an input gate, forget gate, and output gate to control the flow of information into and out of the cell state.
- **GRU (Gated Recurrent Unit):** A simpler variant with only two gates (update and reset) and merges the cell state and hidden state.
