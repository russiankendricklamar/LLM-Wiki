# Merton Jump-Diffusion

[[Merton's Jump-Diffusion]] model adds a log-normal compound Poisson process to standard geometric Brownian motion, capturing heavy tails and volatility smiles: $S_t = S_0 \exp(X_t)$ where $X_t$ includes jumps.
