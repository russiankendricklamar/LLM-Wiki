# Martingale Central Limit Theorem

The [[Martingale CLT]] extends the standard CLT to dependent variables. If $(M_n)$ is a martingale with bounded increments and predictable quadratic variation $\langle M \rangle_n \to \infty$, then $M_n / \sqrt{\langle M \rangle_n} \xrightarrow{d} \mathcal{N}(0,1)$.
