# Vanna & Volga

[[Vanna]] ($\partial^2 V/\partial S \partial \sigma$) and [[Volga]] ($\partial^2 V/\partial \sigma^2$) are second-order options Greeks. They are crucial for FX options pricing to adjust Black-Scholes prices for the volatility smile empirically observed in markets.
