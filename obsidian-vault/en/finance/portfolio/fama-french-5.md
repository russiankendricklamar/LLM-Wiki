# Fama-French 5-Factor

The [[Fama-French 5-Factor Model]] extends the 3-factor model with profitability (RMW) and investment (CMA) factors: $R_i - R_f = \alpha_i + \beta_M(R_M - R_f) + b_{SMB}SMB + b_{HML}HML + b_{RMW}RMW + b_{CMA}CMA + \epsilon_i$.
