# Zero-Knowledge Proofs

[[Zero-Knowledge Proofs]] (ZKPs) allow a prover to convince a verifier that a statement is true without revealing any information beyond its veracity. Systems like zk-SNARKs rely on polynomial commitments and pairing-based cryptography.
