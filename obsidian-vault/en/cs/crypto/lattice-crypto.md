# Lattice-based Cryptography

[[Lattice-based Cryptography]] offers quantum-resistant security based on hard problems like Learning With Errors (LWE) and Shortest Vector Problem (SVP). LWE finds $s$ given $A$ and $b = As + e \pmod q$.
