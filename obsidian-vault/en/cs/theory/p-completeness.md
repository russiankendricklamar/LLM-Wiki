# P-completeness

[[P-completeness]] characterizes problems in P inherently difficult to parallelize. Problems like Circuit Value Problem (CVP) and Linear Programming are P-complete under NC reductions, implying they likely lack $O(\text{polylog}(n))$ time parallel algorithms.
