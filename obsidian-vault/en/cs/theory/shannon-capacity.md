# Shannon Capacity

The [[Shannon Capacity]] sets the limit for error-free transmission. For AWGN channels, $C = B \log_2(1 + \text{SNR})$. For multi-user channels (MAC, Broadcast), capacity regions depend on simultaneous interference limits.
