# Digital Filter Design

[[Digital Filters]] manipulate discrete signals. FIR (Finite Impulse Response) ensures linear phase via symmetry. IIR (Infinite Impulse Response) achieves steep roll-offs with lower order by introducing feedback poles: $H(z) = \frac{B(z)}{A(z)}$.
