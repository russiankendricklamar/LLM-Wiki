# Navier-Stokes Regularity

The [[Navier-Stokes Regularity Problem]] questions whether 3D incompressible viscous fluid equations admit smooth, globally defined solutions. $\partial_t u + (u \cdot \nabla)u = -\nabla p + \nu \Delta u$. It is a Millennium Prize problem.
