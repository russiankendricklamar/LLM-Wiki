# Prime Number Theorem

The [[Prime Number Theorem]] describes the asymptotic distribution of primes: $\pi(x) \sim \frac{x}{\ln x}$ as $x \to \infty$. Its proof relies on the non-vanishing of the Riemann zeta function $\zeta(s)$ on the line $\text{Re}(s) = 1$.
