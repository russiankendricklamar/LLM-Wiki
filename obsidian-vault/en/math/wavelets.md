# Wavelets

[[Wavelets]] provide time-frequency localization, overcoming limits of Fourier transforms. A wavelet basis $\psi_{a,b}(t) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{a}}\psi\left(\frac{t-b}{a}\right)$ allows multiresolution analysis in $L^2(\mathbb{R})$.
