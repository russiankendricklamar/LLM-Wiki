# Queuing Theory

[[Queuing Theory]] models waiting lines. For M/G/1 queues, the Pollaczek-Khinchine formula gives the expected waiting time: $W = \frac{\lambda E[S^2]}{2(1-\rho)}$, linking utilization $\rho$ and service variance to congestion delays.
