# Sobolev Embeddings

[[Sobolev Spaces]] $W^{k,p}$ equip $L^p$ spaces with weak derivatives. The Rellich-Kondrachov theorem proves that certain Sobolev embeddings into lower-order spaces are compact, essential for PDEs.
