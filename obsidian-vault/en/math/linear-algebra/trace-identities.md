## Overview
The trace of a square matrix $A$, denoted $\text{Tr}(A)$, is the sum of its diagonal elements.

## Identities
1. **Cyclic Property:** $\text{Tr}(ABCD) = \text{Tr}(BCDA) = \text{Tr}(CDAB)$
2. **Eigenvalue Sum:** $\text{Tr}(A) = \sum_{i} \lambda_i$
3. **Linearity:** $\text{Tr}(aA + bB) = a\text{Tr}(A) + b\text{Tr}(B)$
4. **Trace of Derivative:** $\frac{\partial}{\partial X} \text{Tr}(AX) = A^T$
