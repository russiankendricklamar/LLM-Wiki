## Overview
The DCT expresses a finite sequence of data points in terms of a sum of cosine functions oscillating at different frequencies.

## Applications
DCT is robust for signal compression (e.g., JPEG, MP3) because it exhibits strong "energy compaction" property: most of the signal information tends to be concentrated in a few low-frequency components.
