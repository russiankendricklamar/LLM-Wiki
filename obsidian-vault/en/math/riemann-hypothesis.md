# Riemann Hypothesis

The [[Riemann Hypothesis]] conjectures that all non-trivial zeros of the Riemann zeta function $\zeta(s)$ lie on the critical line $\text{Re}(s) = \frac{1}{2}$. It tightly bounds the error term in the Prime Number Theorem: $\pi(x) = \text{Li}(x) + O(\sqrt{x}\ln x)$.
