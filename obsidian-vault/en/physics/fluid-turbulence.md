# Fluid Turbulence

Kolmogorov's 1941 theory of [[Fluid Turbulence]] describes the energy cascade in turbulent flows. The energy spectrum follows $E(k) = C \epsilon^{2/3} k^{-5/3}$, independent of viscosity in the inertial subrange.
