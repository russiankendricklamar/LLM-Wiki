# Renormalization Group

The [[Renormalization Group]] (RG) studies how physical systems change across length scales. The Callan-Symanzik equation $\left(\mu \frac{\partial}{\partial \mu} + \beta(g)\frac{\partial}{\partial g}\right)\Gamma = 0$ dictates the flow of couplings, explaining phase transitions.
