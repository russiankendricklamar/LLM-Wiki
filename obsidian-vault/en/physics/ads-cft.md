# AdS/CFT Correspondence

The [[AdS/CFT Correspondence]] is a realization of the holographic principle, equating a theory of gravity in Anti-de Sitter space to a Conformal Field Theory on its boundary. $Z_{CFT}[\phi_0] = Z_{string}[\phi \to \phi_0]$.
