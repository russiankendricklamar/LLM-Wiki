# NLSE Solitons

In fiber optics, [[Solitons]] arise from the balance between group velocity dispersion and self-phase modulation, modeled by the Non-linear Schrödinger Equation (NLSE): $i\partial_z u - \frac{\beta_2}{2}\partial_{tt} u + \gamma |u|^2 u = 0$.
