# Quantum Chromodynamics

[[Quantum Chromodynamics]] (QCD) is the $SU(3)$ gauge theory of strong interactions. The Lagrangian is $\mathcal{L} = -\frac{1}{4}G_{\mu\nu}^a G^{\mu\nu}_a + \bar{\psi}_i(i\gamma^\mu D_\mu - m)\psi_i$. It exhibits asymptotic freedom and color confinement.
