# Hawking-Penrose Theorems

The [[Hawking-Penrose Singularity Theorems]] prove that under specific conditions (e.g., strong energy condition $R_{\mu\nu}k^\mu k^\nu \geq 0$), spacetime singularities are inevitable in General Relativity.
