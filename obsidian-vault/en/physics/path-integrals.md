# Path Integrals in QM

The [[Path Integral Formulation]] expresses quantum amplitudes as a sum over all possible histories. The propagator is $K(x_b, t_b; x_a, t_a) = \int \mathcal{D}x \exp(iS[x]/\hbar)$, where $S$ is the classical action.
