## Overview
The Schrödinger equation is a linear partial differential equation that governs the wave function of a quantum-mechanical system.

## Time-Dependent Equation
$$ i \hbar \frac{\partial \Psi}{\partial t} = \hat{H} \Psi $$
where $i$ is the imaginary unit, $\hbar$ is the reduced Planck constant, $\Psi$ is the state vector of the quantum system, and $\hat{H}$ is the Hamiltonian operator representing the total energy.
