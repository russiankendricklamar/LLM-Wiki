# Solitons

[[Solitons]] are robust, solitary wave packets that maintain their shape during propagation and collisions. Described by non-linear equations like the Korteweg-de Vries (KdV) equation: $\partial_t u + \partial_{xxx} u + 6u\partial_x u = 0$.
