## Overview
The wave equation is an important second-order linear partial differential equation describing waves such as sound, light, and water waves.

## Mathematical Formulation
In 1D, for a scalar function $u(x,t)$ representing the displacement:
$$ \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial t^2} = c^2 \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2} $$
where $c$ is the wave speed. D'Alembert's solution shows that $u(x,t) = f(x-ct) + g(x+ct)$, representing left and right traveling waves.
