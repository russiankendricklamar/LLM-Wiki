## The Four Laws
1. **Zeroth Law:** If two systems are in thermal equilibrium with a third, they are in equilibrium with each other.
2. **First Law (Conservation of Energy):** $\Delta U = Q - W$. Change in internal energy equals heat added minus work done by the system.
3. **Second Law:** The entropy of an isolated system always increases ($dS \ge 0$). Heat cannot spontaneously flow from cold to hot.
4. **Third Law:** As temperature approaches absolute zero, the entropy of a perfect crystal approaches zero.
